The Temple of Spirituality

Modern Architecture with Spiritual Roots

The Swarved Mahamandir is a meditation centre dedicated to the human race — for their moral and spiritual growth. Spanning over 300,000 square feet and crowned by a 125-petal lotus dome, it rises above the fields of Umraha, on the outskirts of the holy city of Varanasi.

It is an amalgamation of ancient philosophy, spirituality and modern architecture — a gathering space for truth-seekers to encounter consciousness beyond physical science.
